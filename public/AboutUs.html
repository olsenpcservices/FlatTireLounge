The Flat Tire Lounge was started by 10 local friends. The building was originally an old feed store when the train tracks were in and recently was a storage building for Perkins Electric.

We started planning what we wanted to do on April 16th and we officially opened on June 4th. We remodeled the inside featuring our bar counter top made with concrete and broken wine bottles polished down for a smooth finish. Our main attraction is our 800 square foot deck that is less than 12 feet from the High Trestle Trail.

The High Trestle Bridge is less than 3 miles west of the Flat Tire Lounge.
